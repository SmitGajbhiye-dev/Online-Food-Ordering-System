## 🗄️ DATABASE DETAILS

### Tables Created
```
USERS (user_id, name, email, password, created_at)
FOOD (food_id, food_name, price, description, created_at)
ORDERS (order_id, user_id FK, total_amount, status, created_at)
ORDER_DETAILS (order_detail_id, order_id FK, food_id FK, quantity, price)
```

### Normalization
- ✅ First Normal Form (1NF) - No repeating groups
- ✅ Second Normal Form (2NF) - No partial dependencies
- ✅ Third Normal Form (3NF) - No transitive dependencies
- ✅ Referential Integrity with Foreign Keys
- ✅ Cascading deletes for data consistency

### Sample Data
- **Users:** 4 sample user accounts configured
- **Food Items:** 5 popular dishes with prices
- **Orders:** 2 sample orders with order details
- **All relationships:** Properly configured
